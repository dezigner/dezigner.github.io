1. Kalpana

Project brief: Bring the experience of Science Centers to Schools.
Outcome: Kalpana- Dome based Low cost Learning Installation for Indian Schools.

Publications: CHI 2013, Paris & ICORD 2013, Chennai

With the idea to bring the experience of science centers to school, I designed and developed a low cost dome based learning installation for Indian schools, ‘Kalpana’. It teaches students a geographic concept, “Sun changes its path in the sky with change in geodesic location and time of the year”. This concept is very difficult to understand with help of school text books.
Students interact with physical setup to see sun’s trajectory visual with supporting audio response. A high fidelity prototype was developed and tested with students.

2. Maplator

Project brief: Improve the process of Mapping for BizTalk Server.
Outcome: Maplator-Empoers business user to perform mapping operations

At: Microsoft,IDC
Business Integration is a process of making systems communicate across standards. Mapping schemas is the most important requirement of the complete integration process. During user studies it was found that the current workflow process of the mapping is linear with a lot of redundant tasks being performed by users due to lack of standards and platforms. Business users have all the knowledge but implementation is done by software developers.
To enhance the workflow process a new method is proposed where business user can perform the mapping operations instead of just documenting. The application is designed such that it integrates in users current workflow and tools used by him. Hi-fidelity prototype was developed using Expressions Blend with retail, digital receipts as mapping content.
